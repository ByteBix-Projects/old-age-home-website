Who We Are

A Home for Those Who Built Our World

Second Inning Social Impact Foundation was born from a simple, powerful belief: the elders who shaped our families, communities, and nation deserve to live their final years with joy, comfort, and respect.

Founded and directed by Pramila Kumari and Pragya Bharti, our foundation is creating a safe, nurturing Old Age Home in Danapur, Bihar — a place where seniors are not just cared for, but truly belong.

We rely on community donations, volunteer time, and the generosity of people like you to make this vision a living reality.

🤝
Dignity First
Every resident is treated with respect and given a voice in their daily life.
🌿
Holistic Wellbeing
Physical, emotional, and spiritual care for complete wellness.
🏡
Community Living
A warm family atmosphere that fights loneliness and isolation.
💡
Transparency
Every donation is accounted for and used with full integrity.

Life at Our Home

What We Provide

Our Old Age Home is designed to feel like a real home — comfortable, loving, and full of life.

🏥

Medical & Healthcare

Regular health check-ups, on-call medical support, nursing care, and medication management for every resident.

🍱

Nutritious Meals

Three balanced, home-cooked meals daily prepared with dietary needs in mind. Festivals celebrated with special food.

🎨

Activities & Events

Yoga, bhajans, cultural events, craft sessions, and outings to keep minds active and spirits high every day.

🛏

Comfortable Stay

Clean, spacious rooms with proper ventilation, attached bathrooms with safety rails, and personal storage space.

💬

Emotional Support

Trained counselors, peer group sessions, and family visit facilitation to nurture mental and emotional wellbeing.

🚑

24/7 Emergency Care

Round-the-clock staff presence with emergency protocols, ambulance access, and hospital liaison at any hour.
